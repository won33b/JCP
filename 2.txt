<h4>Powerpoint Slides</h4>
<p>
	Below you'll find the Powerpoint slides that accompany the 8th edition  of our textbook.
	There are more than 800 slides, covering each chapter  and subsection of the book. These slides were
	extensively updated in  the Spring of 2020 with content updates to match them with the 8th  edition, with
	many more animations throughout, a common "look and feel"  throughout, and a 16:9 aspect ratio for modern
	projectors.
	<br/>
	<br/>
	<span class='font-weight-bold'>A note on the use of these ppt slides.</span> We're making these slides freely
	available to all (faculty, students, readers). They're in Powerpoint form so you see the animations; and can
	add, modify, and delete slides and slide content to suit your needs. They obviously represent a lot of work
	(over more than 20 years!) on our part. In return for use, we only ask the following:
	<ul>
	  <li> If you use these slides (e.g., in a class) that you mention their source. After all, we'd like people to use our book!</li>
	  <li> If you post any slides on a www site, that you note that they are adapted from (or perhaps identical to) our slides, and note our copyright of this material. </li>
	</ul>
	Thanks and enjoy!
</p>
